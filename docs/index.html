How It Works

ENCODE

Quantum State Encoding

Maps population fitness to complex amplitudes via Boltzmann weighting. Each solution becomes a component of a quantum-like superposition.

EVOLVE

Quantum Evolution

Three operators: Rotation (fitness→phases), Entanglement (solution cross-talk via inner products), Scrambling (controlled noise). Creates constructive interference on good solutions.

COLLAPSE

Measurement Collapse

PCA-derived measurement basis + Born rule + entropy constraint = adaptive selection that extracts more information per evaluation than tournament selection.

AUDIT

Cryptographic Trail

Every step is HMAC-SHA256 signed and hash-chained. Tamper with one step → all subsequent signatures break. Full verifiability.
